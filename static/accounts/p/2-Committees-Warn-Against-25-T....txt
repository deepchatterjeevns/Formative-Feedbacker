2 Committees Warn Against 25% Tax Top
Staff economists for two congressional committees reported yesterday that a proposed * 26 percent ceiling on Federal income taxes probably would mean Federal deficits, inflation and Federal sales taxes.</br></br>This appraisal was given in a staff report prepared by economists for the House-Senate Economic Committee and the House Small Business Committee.</br></br>It dealt with a proposal, being put before the State Legislatures, calling for a .constitutional convention to draft an amendment fixing a 25 percent limit on personal and corporate income taxes.</br></br>Under present law, Federal income levies can take up to 88 percent of personal incomes and 70 percent of corporate income.</br></br>The report said 24 State Legislatures have acted on the proposal to ask for the constitutional amendment through a convention process, permitted under the Constitution but never used thus far.
